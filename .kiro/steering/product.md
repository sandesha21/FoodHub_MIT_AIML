# Product Overview

## FoodHub Analysis Project

This is a data analysis project focused on exploring online food delivery data from FoodHub, a food aggregator company operating in New York. The project aims to uncover patterns in restaurant demand, order behavior, and delivery performance to help optimize operations and improve customer satisfaction.

### Key Objectives
- Analyze customer order patterns and trends
- Identify popular restaurants and cuisine types
- Examine delivery performance metrics
- Provide data-driven business recommendations

### Dataset
- **Size**: 1,898 customer orders across 178 restaurants
- **Scope**: New York food delivery data
- **Features**: Order details, restaurant info, delivery metrics, customer ratings
- **Purpose**: Exploratory data analysis for business insights

### Business Value
The analysis supports strategic decision-making for:
- Marketing campaigns and promotions
- Logistics and delivery optimization
- Restaurant partnership strategies
- Customer satisfaction improvements
