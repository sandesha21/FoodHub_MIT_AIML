## üçï Project Overview

This **comprehensive data science and machine learning project** analyzes food delivery data for **FoodHub**, a food aggregator company operating in New York. The analysis provides actionable insights into customer behavior, restaurant performance, and operational efficiency using advanced statistical methods and machine learning algorithms to help improve business operations and customer experience.

**üéØ What makes this project unique:**
- End-to-end data science workflow from EDA to ML deployment
- Statistical hypothesis testing with business validation
- Customer segmentation using advanced analytics
- Revenue optimization through data-driven insights
- Professional-grade visualizations and reporting

### üéØ Business Context & Data Science Application

FoodHub connects customers with multiple restaurants through a single smartphone app, facilitating online food ordering and delivery. This **data science project** applies advanced analytics and machine learning techniques to solve real business challenges in the food delivery industry.

**Key Business Challenges Solved:**
- **Customer Analytics**: Understanding satisfaction patterns using statistical analysis
- **Operational Intelligence**: Optimizing delivery operations through data-driven insights  
- **Performance Analytics**: Identifying high-performing restaurants using ML algorithms
- **Revenue Optimization**: Maximizing revenue through predictive modeling and business intelligence

## üìä Dataset Information

- **Size**: 1,898 orders across 178 restaurants
- **Customers**: 1,200 unique customers  
- **Cuisines**: 14 different cuisine types
- **Time Period**: Representative sample of FoodHub operations

### Data Features
- Order details (ID, cost, cuisine type)
- Customer information and ratings
- Restaurant performance metrics
- Delivery logistics (preparation time, delivery time)
- Temporal patterns (weekday vs weekend)

## üîç Key Analysis Areas

### 1. **Customer Behavior Analysis**
- Rating patterns and customer satisfaction
- Customer segmentation (VIP, frequent, one-time users)
- Engagement metrics and feedback analysis

### 2. **Operational Performance**
- Delivery time analysis with statistical testing
- Weekday vs weekend performance comparison
- Service level assessment (orders >60 minutes)

### 3. **Revenue Optimization**
- Commission structure analysis (25% for >$20, 15% for $5-$20)
- Revenue per order and customer metrics
- Identification of optimization opportunities

### 4. **Restaurant & Market Analysis**
- Top-performing restaurants and cuisines
- Market share analysis
- Restaurant recommendation system for promotions

## üìà Key Findings

### Customer Insights
- **38.8%** of orders remain unrated (major engagement opportunity)
- **74.2%** customer satisfaction rate (4-5 star ratings)
- Clear customer segmentation with identifiable VIP users

### Operational Performance
- **26% longer** delivery times on weekdays (statistically significant)
- **10.54%** of orders exceed 60 minutes (above 5% industry standard)
- Average total delivery time: **49.8 minutes**

### Revenue Analysis
- **Total Revenue**: $6,166.30 from 1,898 orders
- **Average Revenue per Order**: $3.25
- **Revenue Conversion Rate**: 84.5% (orders generating commission)

## üéØ Strategic Recommendations

### Priority 1: Customer Engagement (6 months)
- **Rating Incentive Program**: 5% discount for customers who rate
- **Target**: Reduce unrated orders from 38.8% to <15%
- **Expected ROI**: Improved service quality and customer retention

### Priority 2: Operational Efficiency (3 months)  
- **Weekday Delivery Optimization**: Add 5 drivers during peak hours
- **Investment**: $15K/month | **Expected Return**: $25K/month
- **Target**: Reduce weekday delivery gap from 26% to <10%

### Priority 3: Revenue Growth (4 months)
- **Minimum Order Strategy**: $8 minimum or $2.99 delivery fee
- **Upselling Program**: Push orders above $20 threshold
- **Expected Impact**: 15% increase in average order value
